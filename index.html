Sniffer

With a search that instantly connects them with a list of foods that fit their dog’s specific needs and a comparison tool that takes two foods and finds any overlapping ingredients, Sniffer makes it easy to quickly zero in on allergies and get your dog on the path to a happier, healthier life.
